Hasbro Game Channel 2014.05 - 2014.09

Ubisoft Entertainment was cooperating with Hasbro Company to work out the game platform on XBOX ONE and PS4, which involves the classic board games, such as Monopoly, Risk, Scrabble etc.

My job was working with UI designer to develop the features on client side by web technology, like HTML5, CSS3, AngularJS and Jquery. This product has been released in September 2014. We ensured the platform was released on time even the design had changed several times. I have completed a couple of essential features in this project, such as achievements, friends etc.
